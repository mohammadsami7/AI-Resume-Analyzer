What is ATS and why does it matter? ‚ñº

ATS (Applicant Tracking System) is software used by employers to filter and rank resumes before they reach human recruiters.


Why it matters:

  • Over 90% of large companies use ATS systems
  • ATS scans for specific keywords from job descriptions
  • Poor ATS compatibility can result in automatic rejection
  • Proper formatting and keyword optimization increase your chances

Tips for ATS optimization:

  • Use standard section headings (Experience, Education, Skills)
  • Include relevant keywords from the job description
  • Use simple formatting and avoid complex layouts
  • Save your resume as both PDF and Word formats
